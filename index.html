CoolSculpting Questions and Answers

How Does CoolSculpting Compare to Liposuction?

Liposuction is among the top two most popular cosmetic procedure in America for both men and women, according to the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS). The thing about liposuction is that it is a surgery, which – like any surgical procedures – comes with some risk. Liposuction involves suctioning the fat away using a hollow instrument referred to as a cannula. The cannula is inserted under the skin before a high-powered vacuum is applied to “suck” the fat away.

While liposuction can help you lose dramatic amounts of fat, there is downtime and healing involved before the full effects of the surgery are experienced. In some cases, it can take up to six weeks before you are fully healed.

CoolSculpting is entirely different. With cryolipolysis, no surgery or downtime is required. There is no cutting or anesthesia.

CoolSculpting is, instead, a non-invasive procedure that uses safe and comfortable cooling pads.

The gel pads are transparent and act as a protector for your skin, nerves, and muscles. Like liposuction, a vacuum is attached to the pads to suck the problem fat bulge into a cup. That is when two cooling panels will begin freezing the fat cells permanently.

Within 30 minutes to an hour, the practitioner will remove the application cup and massage the problem area to break up even more fat cells.

Some people read or watch movies on their tablets while undergoing the procedure, and many people return to work when their sessions are complete.

How Long Do CoolSculpting Treatments Last?

Many patients find that they can arrive for their treatments and have the fat frozen away in as little as 30-minutes, depending on the area being treated. Some areas can take up to 60 minutes. Your times may vary, depending on how many areas you wish to treat in any given session.

How Much Fat Can CoolSculpting Help You Lose?

The natural fat loss experienced by CoolSculpting has been shown to reduce up to 25% of fat cells from any given targeted area, in most cases.

How Many Areas Can Be Treated in a Single CoolSculpting Session?

In most cases, two sessions are considered ideal for noticeable and long-lasting results. You may require more or fewer sessions to achieve your desired results.

Are There Any Side Effects?

Some patients can experience minor side effects, such as numbness or redness at the treatment area, as well as swelling and tingling. These side effects will be explained to you during your free initial consultation, where the purpose is to identify if you are a good candidate for this amazing weight loss procedure.
