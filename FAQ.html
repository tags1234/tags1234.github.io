WHAT IS MICROBLADING?

Microblading is the art of implanting pigments at a proper depth with a hand tool, the “microblade”. It is done in such a way to create realistic, crisp hairstrokes for a natural look. Microblading is best for clients who have dry or ‘normal’ skin types because the pigment retains and does not fade and blur as quickly as in clients who have oily or mature skin. A 4-8 week follow-up appointment is required for optimal results.

WHAT IS MACHINE SHADING/OMBRE/POWDER BROW

Machine shading is the technique that uses a light hitting tattoo machine moving back and forth to build layers of color to achieve the desired look you want.
Machine method is best for most clients including those who have large pores, oily, mature, and thin skin types.
Like microblading, this method also requires a 4-8 week touch up.

IS PERMANENT MAKEUP SAFE?

100%! The studio is kept clean at all times to ensure the safety of everyone that steps foot inside. Everything used is one time use, disposable tools and supplies. Every single client is treated like the very first client of the day. This means that each set-up for every new client is discarded safely afterwards. All stations are thoroughly disinfected before opening and after each procedure throughout the day. Pigments used are 100% vegan and cruelty free.
After leaving the studio Blush Brow Bar can not be held responsible for outside factors that may result in less desirable healed results. You will be provided with a proper aftercare kit along with thorough instructions, but it is up to you to follow through. Your satisfaction is our top priority and we want to make sure that you are pleased which is why you will need to come back for a 4-10 week touch-up (depending on the service you have had).

WHAT SHOULD I EXPECT DURING MY TREATMENT?

• We start off with a discussion regarding your desired brow shape and color preference. Your bone structure and lifestyle will play a huge role in determining the best suitable shape. This usually takes about 25-35 min.
• Next we will take all of your face measurements and create your new brow design.
• Once you have approved your new brow design we will apply a topical numbing cream. Numbing takes approximately 20 minutes and then we start the procedure. A medical grade topical numbing agent is applied throughout the procedure to keep you comfortable.
